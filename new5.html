KPR institude of engineering and tecnology

Established in 2009, KPR Institute of Engineering and Technology is located in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu. The students can opt for Full Time UG and PG courses. Students selecting any of the available courses are taught by expert faculty members. The faculty members are trained professionals with a great amount of research and work done in fields such as Civil Engineering, Computer Science Engineering, Information Technology, Mechanical Engineering, Structural Engineering, VLSI Design, Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning, Biomedical Engineering, Chemical Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Electronics & Communication Engineering and Mechatronics Engineering and others. Students joining the KPR Institute of Engineering and Technology must pay the course fee between INR 50,000-2,00,000. The institute has a well-established campus with several facilities supporting the learning zeal of students.

KPR Institute of Engineering and Technology is an Autonomous Engineering Institution located in Arasur, Coimbatore. The Institution was established by KPR Charities in 2009 and is affiliated to Anna University, Chennai. The institute is accredited by NAAC and NBA. The institute has remarkable academic excellence and a significant placement record. The industry-driven Institution is certified by DSIR as the Scientific and Industrial Research Organization. KPRIET Coimbatore offers 8 UG Programs, 4 PG Programs and 6 Ph.D. Programs. The institution also provides hands-on training on life skills through Liberal Arts courses and Foreign Language courses.
